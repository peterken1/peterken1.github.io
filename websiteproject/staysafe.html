STAY SAFE.

We are all happy to buy and use faster and better electronic devices like personal computers, notebooks, and various mobile devices. This is all good, however with this comes a lot of situation where those same devices end up encreasing the chances of having vulnerabilities and we have to update them regularly to beef up security in the devices. The above image shows the various ways that we can have malwares get into our devices. Use strong passwords and make sure to use firewalls and updating software in devices.

  • 1-> It is important to always turn on the anti-virus in our devices. This step helps to keep away virulent software that may try to infect computing devices.
  • 2-> The next most important measure is to avoid clicking on links without making sure that you trust the source of the links. Think twice before clicking on links.
  • 3-> When you receive emails from respectable institutions asking for you to confirm passwords and other sensitive information, always verify the source.
